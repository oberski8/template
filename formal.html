Executive Summary

The discipline of marketing is the action a business takes to promote a product or service with the help of research and advertising. Every major has its own language. The “spherical marking concept,” founded in the 1950’s, is one of the most famous and fundamental concepts developed in marketing literature. It states that marketing “has no start, no end, it’s seamless” (Svensson). Marketing is a part of the very prestigious business field. Michigan State University’s College of Business is globally known and very difficult to get into. Once a student is accepted, there are many different fields of marketing one can study. Students can specialize in marketing research, sales and retail, public relations, product development and many more. All these types of marketing come with exciting change, such as new and emerging technology. A big and obvious change is the impact of social media on marketing. Marketing and business are always changing. Companies are always producing new content and improving their services in ways that gives marketers a chance to communicate that change with the world, visually or verbally. Marketing is the voice of a company and communication is crucial is this type of business. It helps the business grow and that’s what makes it such an exciting major.
