Conclusion

Marketing is the creative and fun part of business. It is the management process in which a product or service is presented to the customer. This gives the people that have the skill of persuasion and leadership the opportunity to shine at what they are good at. Marketing is such a broad discipline and provides many different opportunities to future employees. Michigan State University has one of the top business schools in the nation. It is very competitive to get into and a great place for a marketing major to earn their education. Students have lots of options to study, such as marketing research, retail/sales, public relations, product development and many more. All job titles come with change everyday and new exciting things to research. Just like how technology is constantly changing everyday. Technology is very important to the marketing world because that’s how the world speaks now, through applications on the Internet. Applications such as Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram are great social media sites used by almost every smartphone holder. Software also is a key factor in marketing research and new and improved versions are being released daily. Another fantastic thing about marketing is that it is the voice of the company. A business has a certain image it wants to display to the public and marketers help get that image out with many types of communication. It needs to be a positive image that attracts more customers. Communication that can be verbal, written, or presented by a representative and it’s almost as if marketers are the soul support system or the cheerleaders of the company. A marketer needs to be good with people, creative, and have a persuasive attitude. They are passionate people and love what they are doing so they can make the public love it too. One needs to build a bond with the customers and gain their trust. Marketers become friends with the consumers. Marketing is a great field to go into and it comes with an endless amount of opportunity.
